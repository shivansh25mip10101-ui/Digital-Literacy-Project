# Social Media Responsibility Checklist for College Students

## ✔️ Do's
- Maintain a professional and positive online presence.
- Respect others' opinions and communicate politely.
- Keep your accounts secure with strong passwords and two-factor authentication.
- Share useful, accurate, and meaningful content.
- Manage your time wisely and limit unnecessary scrolling.

## ❌ Don'ts
- Don’t post anything that could harm your reputation or future career.
- Don’t overshare personal details like location, phone number, or passwords.
- Don’t believe or spread fake news without checking sources.
- Don’t engage in online fights, trolling, or cyberbullying.
- Don’t ignore digital wellbeing — avoid addiction to social media.

---

## 📋 Checklist Table

| Do's | Don'ts |
|------|--------|
| Be respectful online | Post harmful or offensive content |
| Secure your account | Share private information |
| Verify information | Spread rumors or fake news |
| Use time wisely | Spend excessive time online |
| Stay positive | Engage in negative interactions |

---

## 💡 Note
Responsible use of social media helps build a good digital identity and protects your personal and professional life.
